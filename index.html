About Kalafhi

Kalafhi Medical Center is a 100% citizen-owned healthcare establishment dedicated to delivering top-notch medical services that are easily accessible to everyone while promoting overall health and wellbeing throughout the nation.

OUR SERVICES

Medical Services

We offer high-quality healthcare services for all ages with cutting-edge diagnostics and treatment options using advanced technology and facilities.

Pharmaceuticals

We provide and supply medical drugs, equipment and services nationwide, to migrate the shortage of medical drugs and equipment in our country.

Physiotherapy

We take a holistic multi-disciplinary approach in patient care management to help regain function and achieve the highest level of independence.

Renewal Aesthetics

Our professionals use non-surgical treatments and products to personalize your skin care for optimal results, helping you look and feel your best.

Day Hospital

Our smart day hospital uses the latest in technology and digital health solutions, offering services such as chemotherapy and cardiology.

Wellness Days

We aim to raise public health awareness and the importance of knowing your health by conducting health screenings for the public and corporations.


Why Choose Kalafhi Medical Centre?

Innovation and Improvement:

Our innovation and improvement motivations stem from the aspiration of discovering and implementing breakthroughs and procedures that will aid our patient's well-being and advance Botswana's healthcare systems.

Patient-centered Care:

The medical care provided at Kalafhi Medical Center is centered around the patient's unique needs and preferences. We aim to focus on providing personalized attention and individualized treatment.

Goal Orientation:

Kalafhi Medical Center is motivated by a collection of principles that shape our actions and steer us towards achieving our objectives. These core values serve as a compass for our conduct and inspire us to strive for prosperity.

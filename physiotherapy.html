Physiotherapy

Kalafhi Medical Centre Physiotherapy takes a holistic multidisciplinary approach in patient care management to help regain function and achieve the highest level of independence. Our aim is to facilitate healing through hands on (manual techniques), exercise prescriptions and electrotherapy to help promote tissue recovery, optimize function and return to desired activity, work or sport.

Kalafhi Medical Centre Physiotherapy understand that you have individual needs and we use a variety of scientific evidence based treatment protocols because your health matters.

We aim to expand our presence in the local communities, contribute meaningfully to the national economy, and provide people with the healthcare and assistance they need and deserve as our comprehensive range of healthcare services caters to individuals of all ages.

MUSCULOSKELETAL DISORDERS

Our physiotherapists specialize in treating injuries and conditions that affect joints, muscles and soft tissues

SPORTS MEDICINE

Our physiotherapists have extensive experience in working with professional and amateur sportsman and have a high success rate of full recovery with return to desired choice of activity.

POST-OPERATIVE REHABILITATION

Kalafhi Medical Centre Physiotherapy have expertise in rehabilitating a variety of post-surgical procedures not limited to; total hip replacement, lumbar laminectomy, knee replacement, ORIF, arthroplasties. We guide you on how to get moving till full function, movement and strength is restored

PULMONARY REHABILITATION

Our physiotherapists can help you recover from chest pain, shortness of breath, fatigue and excessive lung secretions. Through chest physiotherapy, lung function will be restored and respiratory muscles will be strengthened.

Our Referral Network

We have an outstanding referral network of doctors, surgeons and specialists which creates a successful path to full recovery.

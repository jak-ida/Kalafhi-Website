Medical Services

At Kalafhi Medical Center, we are dedicated to serving you and your family with the highest standard of care. Whether you need a routine check-up, treatment for an acute illness, or ongoing management of a chronic condition, our general practitioners are here to support your health journey.


Contact us today to schedule an appointment and experience our commitment to your well-being.

GENERAL PRACTITIONER CONSULTATION


General medical practice, also known as general practitioner (GP) services, forms the foundation of primary healthcare. We see and treat a variety of medical conditions across all age groups. We also offer preventative & routine services such as; diabetes testing, electrocardiogram (ECG) testing, chronic condition care including HIV, hypertension, arthritis, asthma & so much more. Our dedicated team of experienced and qualified general practitioners is committed to delivering personalized care to individuals and families of all ages.

We provide different vaccines for COVID, rabies, tetanus, chicken pox, flu, yellow fever, cervix for HPV & other required vaccinations.

VACCINES

We provide health screens & testing for STI, pregnancy, prostate cancer, cervical cancer & other medical conditions.

We also provide health screenings that include blood pressure checks, cholesterol tests, and immunizations, to keep you on top of your well-being.

HEALTH SCREENINGS

We test for any blood related diseases like STI’s, cholesterol & other medical conditions.

LAB SERVICES

Our patient, compassionate and comprehensive approach to paediatric care ensures your child will feel comfortable and receive the highest quality treatment possible. We offer new-born care, infant care annual physicals, and routine services such as baby weighing and immunizations.

CHILD WELFARE CLINIC

Our skilled general practitioners conduct assessments, perform physical examinations, and utilize their medical expertise to diagnose and treat a variety of common medical conditions. From respiratory infections and skin conditions to minor injuries and digestive problems, we are here to help.

DIAGNOSIS & TREATMENT

We offer medical health examinations for companies, food handlers, as well as pre, periodic & exit employment medical examinations.

MEDICAL EXAMINATIONS

Upon request, our clients can be consulted and treated from the comfort of their home. This service allows for both a physical visit and also provision of remote diagnosis and treatment of patients by means of telecommunications technology.

HOME VISITS &
TELEMEDICINE

If you have a chronic condition such as diabetes, hypertension, or asthma, our general practitioners specialize in ongoing management. They work closely with you to develop personalized treatment plans, monitor your condition, adjust medications, and provide support to enhance your quality of life.

CHRONIC DISEASE
MANAGEMENT

We are committed to distributing knowledge to make informed decisions about your health. Our GPs offer guidance on healthy lifestyle choices, including nutrition, exercise, stress management, and smoking cessation. We believe that prevention and education are key components of optimal health.

& EDUCATION

Our Referral Network

If specialized care is required, our general practitioners have a vast network of trusted specialists whom they can refer you to. We ensure seamless coordination of care to facilitate your journey towards better health outcomes.

Kalafhi Day Hospital

Kalafhi Day Hospital, a flagship initiative of the Kalafhi Group of Companies, is a state-of-the-art day hospital set to launch in September 2023. Located in Gaborone. The hospital's focus will be on outpatient procedures and treatments, enabling patients to return home on the same day as their medical intervention. With a capacity of 26 beds, the hospital is strategically designed for short stays, emphasizing efficiency and convenience for patients.

Coming Soon!

At the core of Kalafhi Day Hospital is the commitment to leverage cutting-edge technology to provide high-quality healthcare. The facility is in advanced discussions with a USA-based organization to integrate robotics-assisted surgery, with the ultimate goal of incorporating Artificial Intelligence (AI)-driven diagnostics and telemedicine services. This emphasis on technological innovation is part of a broader strategy to streamline medical processes, enhance patient experiences, and improve patient outcomes.

The hospital will offer a comprehensive range of healthcare services. Specialties will include General Practice, Internal Medicine, Pediatrics, Obstetrics and Gynecology, Orthopedics, Ophthalmology, Dermatology, Urology, Ear, Nose, and Throat (ENT), Cardiology, Gastroenterology, Neurology, Endocrinology, Oncology, and Pulmonology. In addition to these specialties, the facility will be equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and services, including operating theaters, diagnostics such as CT scans and MRI, physical rehabilitation centers, renal care, and dialysis.

Mission

Kalafhi Day Hospital's mission is to reshape healthcare delivery through a strong emphasis on primary and preventive healthcare. We are committed to utilizing state-of-the-art technology and digital systems to enhance healthcare accessibility and effectiveness, thereby lowering disease burden on our community. By making quality healthcare both affordable and readily accessible, we aim to improve the overall health of our population, thereby enhancing quality of life and encouraging longevity.



Vision

Our vision at Kalafhi Day Hospital is to establish ourselves as a leading healthcare provider, making quality healthcare not only accessible but also affordable for all, even the lowest income earners. We envision a healthcare system primarily centered on preventive measures and preemptive care, enabling early disease detection and intervention. Our commitment extends to reducing the reliance on tertiary care as far as possible. By incorporating advanced technology and digital solutions, we aim to make primary healthcare scalable and accessible, positioning ourselves at the forefront of medical innovation and striving to make Botswana a recognized regional hub for medical tourism.

Guided by its mantra - patient first, innovation always, global impact next - Kalafhi Day Hospital is committed to making healthcare accessible, affordable, and of high quality. The vision is to become a leading healthcare provider, focusing on primary and preventive care, with the goal of detecting and managing diseases early, thereby reducing the need for tertiary care.

Through its innovative approach, Kalafhi Day Hospital aims to make a significant impact on the local and global healthcare landscape, enhancing the health and wellbeing of the community it serves. The hospital seeks collaboration with medical professionals and healthcare service providers who share its vision and commitment to transforming healthcare.

Our Values

We are dedicated to delivering high-quality healthcare services that meet the unique needs of every individual.

We believe in a healthcare system where everyone, irrespective of their socio-economic status, has access to the care they need.

We strive to stay at the forefront of medical advancements, integrating cutting-edge technology and digital solutions to improve our services and patient experiences.

We champion a healthcare approach that emphasizes preventive care and early detection, promoting healthier lives and reduced disease burden.

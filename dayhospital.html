Kalafhi Day Hospital

KALAFHI'S DAY HOSPITAL

Our smart day hospital is a 12 bed facility which uses the latest in technology and digital health solutions, offering the use of 2 theatres and services such as Chemotherapy and Cardiology, Radiology, Oncology, Opthamology and much more!

Benefits of a Day Hospital:

• Quick Turnaround Time;
• Less recovery time through the use of minimal invasive procedures;
• Affordability and accessibility - Quick to attain in the case of emergencies;
• They act as a step down facility for major hospitals;
• They complement the major hospitals;

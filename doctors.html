Meet The Team

Kalafhi Medical Centre is committed to improving the quality of healthcare provided, and achieving this by building a network of highly experienced doctors from various walks of life. This diverse network of doctors brings together top minds to work towards improving and uplifting patient care.

At Kalafhi, patient care takes center stage, and the team values the importance of teamwork to ensure the best possible care for patients. The Fri:endly atmosphere at Kalafhi fosters a supportive environment where staff can share their knowledge and skills, building strong relationships, and enhancing patient care.

Head Doctors

Dr. Ezekiel Dono Maine

Specialization: General Practitioner

Head Doctor at Main Mall

Dr. Maine is a General Practitioner with 43 years of experience at Princess Marina Hospital, Deborah Relief Memorial Hospital and as a private doctor. He loves the art of medicine and aims to continue working in the field as long as the good Lord gives him the strength to do so. He is grateful to Kalafhi for giving him a wonderful opportunity to continue his profession.

Dr. Motena Selepeng-Maema

Specialization: General Practitioner

Head Doctor at Headquatrters

Dr. Selepeng-Maema holds a degree in Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery from the University of London. She is a practicing doctor at Kalafhi Medical Center, and is a member of the Royal College of Physicians. She has worked at multiple hospitals in the United Kingdom, and has over 30 years of work experience.

Dr. Mpho Alphonce Setime

Specialization: General Practitioner

Head Doctor at Tlokweng

Dr. Setime holds a degree in Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery and a Masters of Business Administration from the Medical School of Southern AFri:ca. He is a practicing doctor at Kalafhi Medical Center. He has worked at multiple hospitals in the country and has over 17 years of work experience.

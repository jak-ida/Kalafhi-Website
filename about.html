ABOUT KALAFHI

Kalafhi Medical Center is 100% citizen-owned company healthcare establishment dedicated to delivering top-notch medical services that are easily accessible to everyone while promoting overall health and wellbeing throughout the nation.


Ever since our inception in 2018, we have aimed to expand our presence in the local communities, contribute meaningfully to the national economy, and provide people with the healthcare and assistance they need and deserve. Our network currently includes 4 branches situated in and around Gaborone, and our comprehensive range of healthcare services caters to individuals of all ages.

Our Pillars

Efficient turnaround time, convenience, and good customer service are essential elements for any successful business, and our company is no exception.


We acknowledge that time is a valuable asset in today's fast-paced world, and we aim to offer our customers prompt and effective service. We also understand the significance of convenience and have made it our objective to ensure that our services are user-friendly and easily accessible.


Our priority is to provide excellent customer service by prioritizing our patients' needs and exceeding their expectations. These fundamental pillars are the bedrock of our business and inform our decision-making in all aspects of our operations.

Our Values

At our company, we are driven by a set of core values that form our behavior and guide us in our quest for success.

Respect is one of the values we hold dear, and we believe that every individual, regardless of their role or position, deserves to be treated with dignity and fairness. We also value integrity, which means that we uphold the highest ethical standards in all our interactions with our clients, employees, and partners. Compassion is another essential value that guides us, and we are committed to providing care that is not only effective but also kind and empathetic.


We believe that innovation is key to staying ahead in today's rapidly changing and fast-paced world, and we encourage creativity and out-of-the-box thinking to drive progress and growth. This in conjunction with teamwork, another crucial value, is central to our operations, and we recognize that collaboration and communication are essential to achieving our goals.


Healing is a multifaceted process, and we are committed to providing comprehensive care that addresses the physical, emotional, and mental well-being of our clients. Finally, we strive for excellence in all our endeavors, and we hold ourselves to the highest standards to ensure that we deliver the best possible service to our clients.


By upholding these values, we aim to create a culture of accountability, integrity, and compassion that drives our success and enables us to make a positive impact on the world around us, one patient at a time, because to us, your health matters.

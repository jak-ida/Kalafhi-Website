About Kalafhi

Kalafhi Medical Center is a 100% citizen-owned healthcare establishment dedicated to delivering top-notch medical services that are easily accessible to everyone while promoting overall health and wellbeing throughout the nation.

We aim to expand our presence in the local communities, contribute meaningfully to the national economy, and provide people with the healthcare and assistance they need and deserve as our comprehensive range of healthcare services caters to individuals of all ages.

Our network currently includes 4 branches situated in and around Gaborone. In our efforts to assert Botswana’s ambition of becoming a health tourism destination of choice, our Clinic aims to expand into and create a hospital.

This hospital will become a healthcare hub in the region which offers a wide variety of services including cardiology, neurology, operation theatres, oncology and will also include visiting specialists to provide services not readily available among others.

Our Pillars

Efficient turnaround time, convenience, and good customer service are essential elements for any successful business, and our company is no exception.

We acknowledge that time is a valuable asset in today's fast-paced world, and we aim to offer our customers prompt and effective service. We also understand the significance of convenience and have made it our objective to ensure that our services are user-friendly and easily accessible.

Our priority is to provide excellent customer service by prioritizing our patients' needs and exceeding their expectations. These fundamental pillars are the bedrock of our business and inform our decision-making in all aspects of our operations.

Our Values

Respect is one of the values we hold dear, and we believe that every individual deserves to be treated with dignity and fairness. We uphold the highest ethical and emphathetic standards in all our interactions with our clients, employees, and partners through our integrity and compassion.

We believe that innovation is key to staying ahead in today's rapidly changing and fast-paced world, and we encourage creativity and out-of-the-box thinking to drive progress and growth and improvement within our clinic. This optimizes our operations as we recognize that the above as well as collaboration and communication are essential to achieving our goals.

By upholding these values, we aim to create a culture that drives our success and enables us to make a positive impact on the world around us, one patient at a time, because to us, your health matters.

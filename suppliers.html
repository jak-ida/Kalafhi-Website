SCOPE

Licamore Pty Ltd invites suitably qualified companies that are interested in providing the following products and services to submit their proposals:

  1. 1. Accommodation and hospitality services
  2. 2. Security systems
  3. 3. Signage, branding services, banners and outdoor displays
  4. 4. Printing services
  5. 5. General maintenance services (Aircons, plumbing, mechanical and electrical office repairs)
  6. 6. Sanitation and refuse disposal services
  7. 7. Storage services
  8. 8. Stationary and Office equipment
  9. 9. Computers, Laptops, and accessories as well as repair of services of the same
  10. 10. Equipment calibration and services
  11. 11. Cleaning services and cleaning detergents
  12. 12. General Supplies
  13. 13. Corporate clothing and promotional material
  14. 14. Stationary, consumables and office equipment
